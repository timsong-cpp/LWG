Library Proposals Status List (Revision 1) DRAFT

This status list tracks the progress of post-C++11 Library proposals. See the current Call for Library Proposals for information about how to submit a proposal to the C++ standards committee.

As projects emerge, Study Groups may be formed to shepherd the proposals into a final deliverable form, such as producing a candidate TS document. This stats list aims to track the progress of proposals through those study groups as well.

Status

Status Description
New A proposal that is awaiting review by the appropriate WG or SG.
Open A proposal that has been reviewed and is awaiting WG or SG action.
Ready A proposal ready for a formal vote. Note: this is normally an intra meeting status.
Adopted A proposal that has been formally accepted for inclusion in an official working paper.
Revised A proposal that has new document that is awaiting review.
Hold A proposal that has been reviewed and is now tabled pending the receipt of a revised proposal document.
Rejected A proposal that does not have sufficient support to move forward and will not be given further consideration.
Future A proposal that does not have sufficient support to move forward in this revision cycle of the standard, but may be of interest for a future standard or technical specification.
